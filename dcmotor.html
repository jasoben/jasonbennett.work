Exploring Electromagnetism via a Papercraft Maker Project, Augmented Reality Videos, Unity Simulations, and 3D Animation

The Papercraft DC Motor is a learning module for middle and high schools that combines die-cutting, electronics, AR videos, animations, and a Unity Simulation to explore electromagnetism. The goal was to create a module on electromagnetism and motors that doesn't require students or teachers to purchase $10-20 kits for each student. The papercraft motor can be built for $.50 / student, and teaches students functional building skills instead of the "plastic snap together" method of most kits.

It was tested successfully in local middle schools.
