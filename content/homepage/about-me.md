That's me with my dog Ursa in the Blue Ridge Mountains. I live in Charlottesville, VA where I enjoy hiking, [drumming](https://soundcloud.com/vivaluchadora), skateboarding, gardening, meditating, trail running, and doing nothing.  

A significant part of my work is imagining how to make immersive learning available and useful for diverse sets of learners. I strive to keep equity, inclusion, anti-racism, and anti-sexism foregrounded in my thinking for every project. 

If you'd like to collaborate, please feel free to reach out!
